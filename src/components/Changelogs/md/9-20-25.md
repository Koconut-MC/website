You may invite anyone to the discord server to play using: https://discord.gg/koco

- Bump from Java 24 to Java 25 as the recommendation for playing
- Server playtime ranks (1 hour - GAYMER, 10 hours - PROGAYMER, 20 hours - EPICGAYMER, 30 hours - ELITE, 50 hours - ENGINEER, 100 hours - NERD, 500 hours - _secret_)
